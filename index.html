About Us

Divine charity is a non-profit organization established since 2021 with the aim of providing charity services to help people in need of quality education, zero hunger and medical rescue. We strongly believe that everyone deserves the right to gain a propoer education and live a healthy life with zero hunger and healthcare access. We envision every child grows with equal opportunities by raising funds for them and gathering services in addition with medical volunteers to diminish the poverty rate in Malaysia.
